About Us

A unique concept of finding Hindi & Bengali fresh music talent (composer & singing) and nurturing them to place before the world audience. The show is conceptualized by Mr. Mahesh Jalan and produced by well known production house M/s. Spicy Entertainment and Media Limited. This show will not only find new talents in music composing & singing from Bengal but also help them to showcase their talent by different mediums.

Muzic4India is a first-of-its-kind, regional singing competition specifically for budding singers in the Eastern Region of India. This fresh music compsing & singing competition aim to attract over 2000 acts as we invite registration from all across the eastern region of India in search for the `Banglar New Talent’. Acts can perform either covers or original material and the Muzic4India singing competitions are open to all genres, so whether you’re into Bollywood, Pop, Classical, Disco, etc. Muzic4India - fresh music composer & singer competition wants YOU!

AGE LIMIT

  • NO UPPER AGE LIMIT. ALL ARE WELCOME ABOVE AGE 18 YEARS.

All types of music composer & singer, aged above 18 are invited to enter Muzic4India - fresh music composer & singing competition. The composer & singing competition main aim is to discover an act that has the ability to become a professional recording artist. If you think you have the talent, the Muzic4India - fresh music composer & singing competition is open to you. Please complete the application form on the website or offline to enter fresh music composing & singing contest Muzic4India now.

PARTICIPANTS

Its a competition for fresh music composer & singer. 

  • Same person can be composer and singer OR
  • A combination of two persons or more: where minimum one composer and one singer is there.
  • Only composer or only singer can also participate (subjected to form a group of composer and singer from our side).
COMPETITION FORMAT
  • Two months Audition Round.
  • After audition final rounds shall last for Three months.
  • First Month - No Elimination.
  • Second Month onwards - Weekly elimination till final week.
  • Top 50 will be selected for final rounds.
  • Top 10 will go for Grand Finale - Live Stage Show
JUDGES

Get your music heard by the right people by showcasing your talent to the music industry. The Muzic4India - fresh music composer & singing competition gives contestants the chance to perform live in front of music and entertainment industry judges, getting more prestigious guests as the competition progresses. Three renowned judges from the music and film industry. Names will be announced soon.

The Muzic4India - fresh music composer & singing competition judges will be looking at vocal/musical talent, stage performance, star quality and individuality. Throughout the journey, acts will have to prove their professionalism and determination and make every performance their own. The Muzic4India - fresh music composer & singing competition judges will be seeking an act that has the potential to be successfully launched in Kolkata as a major recording artist. Therefore motivational skills are essential who can promote themselves will have a greater chance of winning.

Competition judging at the live showcases will not only be based on vocal ability, but also image, performance, originality and star quality, with an overall view to find a recording artist suitable for composing music and playback singing. Online Voting, Downloads, SMS voting and Judges’ Points altogether will decide the Winner.

If you're successful in your audition you will be given a place in Final Rounds and the opportunity to perform live to capacity audiences at some of the best venue in the city. Final Rounds will include all the best unsigned acts in your region and give acts the important chance to showcase their talents to a music industry judging panel as well as a huge live crowd!

It's here that you’ll have the chance to get noticed by the music industry not only in Tollywod but in Bollywood as well.

THE MUSIC COMPOSING & SINGING COMPETITION REWARD

  • Chance to make a Video Album for Top 3 contestants.
  • Memento of Participation for all Final Round contestants
  • Gifts and Prizes for Semi Final and Final round contestants.
  • Free Holiday Trip for the Winner
  • Chance of doing Live Show in front of live audience and prestigious guests at renowned venue like Kalamandir.
  • Chance of getting break in bollywood and tollywood for playback singing.
  • The fresh music composing & singing singing competition winner will be crowned the winner of Muzic4India in front of a capacity crowd, including industry insiders, celebrity guests, national press, etc.
